Skills and Experience

User Research

Each case always beings and ends with the most important factor in mind: the user.

Using information and data from our stakeholders, we drafted our proto-personas to serve as a beginning hypothesis. More often than not, we found our assumptions challenged or debunked upon our actual user interviews and tests being conducted.

Despite the challenges of finding, interviewing and testing users all via online networking and Zoom calls during this year's shelter-in-place orders, me and my teams always went above and beyond in finding and learning from users about their actual needs.

On top of our interviews and tests, me and my teams built targeted online surveys via Google Forms and visualized our data in Miro into core points to address in our cases.
